\section{Data Integration}\label{sec:si_data}

We integrate two sources of US state-level pneumonia-and-influenza
weekly death counts: CDC FluView, covering 2010--2018, and
HealthData.gov, covering 2017--2025. The two sources overlap in
2017--2018. In the overlap period we take, for each (state, week)
pair, the simple arithmetic mean of the two reported death counts;
outside the overlap, the value is taken from whichever source covers
it. The two sources agree closely in the overlap period.
